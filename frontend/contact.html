Frequently Asked Questions

How can I modify my booking?

You can modify your booking through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team. Changes are subject to hotel availability and policies.

Do you offer refunds?

Refund policies vary by hotel and booking type. Most bookings offer free cancellation up to 24-48 hours before check-in.

Can I pay on arrival?

Payment options depend on the hotel. Some properties accept payment on arrival, while others require advance payment during booking.
